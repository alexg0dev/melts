Our Process

See How We Craft Our Soaps

Every Melissa's Melts product is handcrafted with care and attention to detail. Our traditional soap-making process preserves the natural benefits of our premium ingredients.

1

Selecting Premium Ingredients

We carefully source the finest natural oils, butters, and essential oils from sustainable suppliers around the world.

2

Cold Process Method

We use the traditional cold process method that preserves the natural benefits and creates a gentle, moisturizing soap.

3

Curing & Quality Control

Each soap is cured for 4-6 weeks and undergoes rigorous quality testing before packaging to ensure perfection.

4

Eco-Friendly Packaging

We wrap each product in sustainable, plastic-free packaging that's kind to the environment and beautifully presented.
