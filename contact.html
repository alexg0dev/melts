Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the UK. International shipping can take 7-14 business days, depending on your location and customs processing. We offer expedited shipping options at checkout for faster delivery.

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We love creating custom products for special occasions. Whether you need wedding favors, party gifts, or a unique scent combination, we're happy to work with you. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote and timeline.

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?

Most of our products are vegan and all are cruelty-free. We clearly label any products that contain honey, milk, or other non-vegan ingredients. All of our products are made without animal testing and we're committed to ethical sourcing practices.

How should I store my soaps and wax melts?

Keep soaps in a dry, well-ventilated area between uses with a soap dish that allows water to drain. Store wax melts in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent melting and preserve the fragrance. Properly stored, our products will maintain their quality for up to a year.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unopened and unused products. Due to the nature of our handcrafted items, we cannot accept returns on opened products unless they arrived damaged. Please contact us if you receive a damaged item and we'll make it right.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees or import taxes that may apply. Contact us if you have questions about shipping to your specific location.
