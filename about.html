The Beginning

Melissa's Melts began in 2019 as a creative outlet in the founder's small kitchen. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion as friends and family couldn't get enough of the handcrafted soaps and wax melts.

"I started making soaps because I wanted products that were kind to my sensitive skin and kind to the planet. I never imagined it would grow into what it is today, but I'm grateful for every customer who has joined us on this journey."

— Melissa Johnson, Founder

From a small market stall to an online store, we've grown steadily, but our commitment remains the same: creating luxurious, handcrafted soaps using only the finest natural ingredients while providing exceptional customer service.

Our Values

What We Stand For

At Melissa's Melts, our mission goes beyond creating beautiful soaps. We're committed to values that guide everything we do.

Natural Ingredients

We believe what you put on your skin matters. That's why we use only natural, ethically sourced ingredients free from harmful chemicals.

  • Organic base oils
  • Pure essential oils
  • No synthetic fragrances

Sustainability

From our ingredients to our packaging, we make choices that minimize our environmental impact and promote a healthier planet.

  • Plastic-free packaging
  • Biodegradable materials
  • Minimal waste production

Handcrafted With Love

Each product is carefully handcrafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality. We put our heart into everything we make.

  • Small batch production
  • Attention to detail
  • Quality over quantity

Our Process

How We Craft Our Soaps

Every bar of soap and wax melt goes through an artisanal process that ensures quality, beauty, and effectiveness.

Step 1

Ingredient Selection

We carefully source the finest natural ingredients, from organic base oils to pure essential oils and botanicals, ensuring quality in every component.

Ethically sourced materials
Step 2

Recipe Formulation

Each product begins with a precise recipe developed through research and testing to create the perfect balance of nourishing properties and sensory experience.

Scientifically tested formulas
Step 3

Small Batch Production

We craft every product in small batches, allowing us to maintain close control over quality and give each item the attention it deserves.

Handcrafted with care
Step 4

Curing & Quality Control

Our soaps cure for 4-6 weeks, allowing excess water to evaporate and creating a mild, long-lasting bar. Every item undergoes quality checks before packaging.

Rigorous quality standards
Step 5

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Finally, we wrap each product in sustainable, plastic-free packaging that protects the product while minimizing environmental impact.

Environmentally conscious
